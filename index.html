Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Connectify Media.

What types of clients do you work with? +

We work with brands of all sizes — from emerging startups to established enterprises. Our sweet spot is growth-stage brands who want to build a serious market presence through strategic events and digital marketing.

How does your event curation process work? +

We start with a discovery session to understand your brand, goals, and audience. From there we develop a full event concept — venue, experience design, vendor coordination, content strategy, and on-site management — delivering a seamless experience from concept to execution.

What does a typical digital marketing engagement look like? +

Most digital engagements are 3–6 months minimum to see meaningful results. We start with an audit and strategy, then move into content creation, paid media, social, and email execution — with monthly reporting and strategy check-ins throughout.

Do you work with clients outside of Canada? +

Absolutely. While we're based in Canada, we work with clients across North America and internationally. For events, we have a trusted network of partners in major cities across the US, UK, and beyond.

How do I get started with Connectify? +

The easiest way is to reach out through our contact page. Tell us a bit about your brand and what you're looking to accomplish, and we'll set up a no-obligation discovery call to explore how we can help.

What makes Connectify different from other agencies? +

We sit at the intersection of live experience and digital marketing — most agencies do one or the other. Our integrated approach means your event generates digital content, your digital campaigns drive event attendance, and everything works together to build a brand people genuinely connect with.
