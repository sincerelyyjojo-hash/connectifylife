Peak Brand Digital Campaign

ClientPeak Brand
Duration6 Months
Year2024–2025
ServicesFull Digital Suite

Their Story

Peak Brand is an apparel and lifestyle company with a loyal but relatively small customer base. They had great products and strong word-of-mouth, but their digital presence didn't reflect the quality of what they were building. Their social channels were inconsistent, their email list was underutilized, and their paid ads were running without a clear strategy.

When they came to us, their cost per acquisition was high, their email open rates were at industry-low 18%, and they were essentially invisible in search. They needed a full digital reset — and they needed it fast.

The Challenge

The brief was ambitious: rebuild their digital presence from the ground up, with measurable ROI as the non-negotiable outcome. Budget was moderate, which meant we needed to be strategic about where we invested effort — no spray-and-pray approach.

We also needed to unify messaging across every channel. Peak's brand voice was inconsistent — playful on Instagram, stiff on email, absent on search — and that fragmentation was costing them credibility and conversions.

Our Approach

We started with a full digital audit across social, email, SEO, and paid — mapping what was working, what was wasted, and what was missing. The findings shaped a 6-month roadmap with clear phases: audit and strategy, foundation rebuild, launch and amplify, optimize and scale.

For social, we rebuilt their content calendar with a clear visual system and a content mix that balanced educational, inspirational, and product-forward posts. For email, we rewrote their welcome sequence, segmented their list by purchase behavior, and rebuilt their monthly newsletter from scratch — turning a neglected channel into their highest-ROI touchpoint.

On paid media, we restructured their Meta campaigns around audience segments rather than single broad campaigns — and introduced TikTok as a new awareness channel with a creator-led content strategy. SEO work focused on long-tail content and product page optimization.

The Results

By month six, the numbers told a clear story. Email open rates rose from 18% to 42%. Lead volume tripled. Cost per acquisition dropped by 52%. Social engagement increased 280% across platforms. Their TikTok presence — which didn't exist when we started — was generating 80,000+ monthly views within three months.

Peak Brand entered the next fiscal year with a digital foundation that could scale — and a team that finally understood what their marketing was doing for them.

"The digital strategy they built for us doubled our follower growth in three months and our email open rates jumped from 18% to 42%. These guys know what they're doing."

— Ollie Barnes, Founder at Peak Brand
