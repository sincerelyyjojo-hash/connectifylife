The Challenge

Sarah Shao Real Estate Team had already built a strong reputation within the Chinese-speaking community across the GTA — a loyal client base, strong referrals, and genuine expertise in serving that market. But the western market was largely untouched.

The challenge was clear: how do you translate a trusted brand from one cultural context to another without losing what makes it authentic? For Sarah Shao's team, it wasn't about starting over — it was about expanding reach while staying true to their identity.

Our Approach

We focused on two channels that could build credibility and visibility simultaneously: short-form video content and curated events.

On the content side, we developed a strategy around reels and carousel posts — formats that perform well across both Instagram and Facebook, and that allow Sarah Shao's expertise, personality, and track record to come through in a format western audiences engage with. Every piece was crafted to feel genuine: not a translation of Chinese-market content, but original content that reflected the team's voice for a new audience.

For events, we helped create moments where Sarah Shao's team could connect directly with clients and prospects — the kind of in-person credibility building that closes the gap between a brand you see online and an agent you trust with one of the biggest decisions of your life.
