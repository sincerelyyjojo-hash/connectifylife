About the Event

The Waterlily Family Fun Day was a family-oriented community celebration hosted by Connectify Media in partnership with LingoAce. Designed as a joyful, inclusive day out for families, the event brought together children and parents for a shared afternoon of fun, play, and connection.

Connectify Media handled event curation and production — creating an environment where families could relax, kids could explore, and the LingoAce community could come together in person. Events like this are at the heart of what we do: creating real-world experiences that build genuine community bonds.
