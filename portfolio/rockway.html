The Partnership

Rockway Vineyards is a beloved winery and golf course nestled in the heart of Niagara Falls, Ontario — one of Canada's most celebrated wine regions. With stunning scenery, award-winning wines, and a loyal local following, Rockway had a story worth telling. Connectify Media was brought in to help tell it.

Our Approach

We created authentic promotional and UGC-style content designed specifically for Rockway's own social channels — the kind of content that feels native to the platform and genuinely reflects the experience of visiting the vineyard. Rather than polished advertising, we focused on producing content that was warm, immersive, and shareable.

The goal was to give Rockway content that their own audience could connect with — content that captured the atmosphere of the vineyard in a way that makes people want to book a visit and experience it themselves.
